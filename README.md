Welcome to the **JIU Student Information Portal**, a centralized digital hub designed specifically to streamline both academic and non-academic campus life for students and administrators. 

This portal was built with a vision to **centralize** scattered information into a single, modern, fast, and easy-to-use access point, available seamlessly on both desktops and mobile devices.

---

## 🎯 Goals & Solutions Offered

Often, students struggle to find academic guidelines, service forms, the latest academic calendars, or scholarship information because they are scattered across various platforms or chat groups. 

**This portal aims to solve these issues by providing:**
1. **A Single Source of Truth:** Students no longer need to ask various departments for forms, guidelines, or announcements. Everything is available right here.
2. **Easy & Integrated Access:** Leveraging Google Workspace Single Sign-On (SSO), students and staff can securely log in using their official campus emails (`@jiu.ac.id`).
3. **Student Independence:** Enhancing campus service efficiency by empowering students to independently search for and download the information they need at any time.
4. **Effortless Management (For Admins):** Campus staff do not need any coding skills to update the website. A dedicated **Admin Dashboard** allows staff to upload PDFs, remove outdated documents, or link new announcements with just a few clicks.

---

## ✨ Key Features

The portal is divided into several main pillars to cover the entire student lifecycle:

### 1. 📚 Academics
- **Academic Calendar:** A comprehensive, interactive schedule to track course registration periods, midterm/final exams, and holidays.
- **Academics Guidelines & Policies:** A collection of student handbooks, academic regulations, and thesis guidelines.
- **Departments:** Specific documents for each department/faculty (such as curricula, class schedules, or major-specific materials).

### 2. 🌍 Campus Life & External Programs
- **Student Affairs:** A hub for Student Organizations (BEM), Student Activity Clubs (UKM), and other student affairs information.
- **External Activities:** Updates on off-campus events, seminars, or competitions.
- **Scholarships, Exchange & Internships:** A dedicated notice board for scholarship opportunities, study abroad exchange programs, and final-year internships.

### 3. 🛠 Services & Finance
- **Cost of Attendance:** Detailed tuition fee information and payment guidelines.
- **Dormitory & Library:** Dormitory rules, registration announcements, and quick access to the e-library catalog.
- **Counseling:** Information on student counseling and wellness services.
- **Download Forms:** A repository of forms (e.g., leave of absence, scholarship applications) ready to be downloaded and printed.
- **Feedback & Suggestions:** A direct channel for students to provide feedback to the university.

### 4. ⚙️ Admin Dashboard
- **Automated Document Management System:** Every pillar above features its own management panel. Admins can effortlessly **Upload** new PDF files, **Edit** document titles, and **Delete** obsolete records.
- The admin interface is designed to be uniform and hassle-free, ensuring that any uploaded documents instantly go live on the student-facing pages.

---

## 📱 User Experience (UX)
- **Premium & Modern Design:** Features a clean interface, highly readable typography, and a visually pleasing color palette.
- **Fully Responsive (Mobile-Friendly):** The website automatically adapts to smartphones and tablets. The sidebar neatly folds away, and tables adjust to fit the screen.
- **In-Browser PDF Viewer:** Students don't need to constantly download files just to peek at their contents. A built-in modal popup allows for seamless PDF reading directly within the browser.
